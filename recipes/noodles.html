Noodles

Description:

Veg noodles aka Vegetable noodles is an easy to make Indo-Chinese dish that serves as a quick dinner or meal option. Made with noodles of choice, some basic vegetables, a hint of black pepper and your favorite sauce, this vegetable noodle dish is flavor disguised in simplicity.

    Ingredients:
  • 2 hakka noodles
  • hot water
  • 2 tsp oil
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 inch gigner, finely chopped
  • 2 chilli
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • ½ carrot, julienne
  • ½ cup cabbage, julienne
  • 5 beans, chopped
  • ½ capsicum, julienne
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 tsp chilli sauce
  • 2 tsp tomato sauce
  • 2 tsp schezwan sauce
  • 2 tsp vinegar
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp pepper powder
  • spring onion, chopped
